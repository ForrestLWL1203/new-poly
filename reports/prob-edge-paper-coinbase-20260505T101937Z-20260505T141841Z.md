## Order No Fill

There were 11 paper no-fill events:

```text
entry no-fill: 6
exit no-fill: 5
```

All no-fills had the same broad shape:

```text
paper ... no fill; retry skipped: signal no longer valid
```

Interpretation:

- The original strategy signal was valid.
- Paper waited the configured simulated latency of about `400ms`.
- The refreshed strategy snapshot no longer confirmed the same entry/exit.
- The retry was skipped instead of chasing stale liquidity.

These are not process errors. In live mode, similar cases are where real FAK
orders may return no match. Exit no-fills deserve continued monitoring because
they can leave a position open longer than intended.
